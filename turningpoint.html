Turning Point

2022 Global Gamejam

The Project's Goal

For the game jam, I wanted to make a linear level with unique feeling areas while having a limited set of assets and improve my leadership and management skills.

What I Learned

It's hard to rush ideas through production in 2 days but we took 4 hours for preproduction on the first day which helped us stay on the same page throughout the entire jam and everyone was thankful that we did it. I found that checking in on people periodically helps them open up and ask more questions versus waiting for them to come and ask. Our game used the jam's theme of Duality by having the player run through the level during the first half of the game avoiding the enemies until they come across the final area where they collect a power-up that lets them turn on the enemies hunting them. The player then runs back through the level killing the enemies to make it back to the start.

Assets

For this project I used: Low Poly Style Deluxe 2: Tropical Environment by WolfDigitalLLC - Unreal Marketplace | interactive stylized Lowpoly Grass by Project Nature - Unreal Marketplace | VFX Bundle 2 by SoerGame - Unreal Marketplace
